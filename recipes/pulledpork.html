Pulled Pork Recipe

Description

A crowd favorite, and it's so simple to make.

Ingredients:

  • Pork shoulder aka pork butt
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup kosher salt
  • 4 tablespoons smoked paprika
  • 2 tablespoons coarse black pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon cumin
  • 1/2 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • Mustard
  • 3 carrots
  • 2 stalks of celery
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 8 ounces of mushrooms
  • 1-2 tbsp of rosemary or thyme

Steps:

  1. Trim excessive fat from the pork shoulder.
  2. Mix dry ingredient to make a rub.
  3. Slather the pork shoulder in mustard - this will act as a binder.
  4. Cover the pork shoulder with the rub.
  5. Set the smoker to 250F.
  6. Place the pork shoulder on the smoker, fat side up.
  7. Once there is a nice bark on the outside of the pork shoulder, remove it from the smoker and wrap it. Before wrapping, add moisture and more rub.
  8. Cook until the internal temperature is between 200-205F.
  9. Remove from the smoker and let it rest anywhere from 30-120 minutes.
  10. Pull it apart and serve it with your favorite coleslaw.
