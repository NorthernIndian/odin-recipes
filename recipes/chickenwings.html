Smoked Chicken Wings Recipe

Description

This is a great appitizer to share with friends when you're watching the game.

Ingredients:

  • A couple pounds of chicken wings
  • Your favorite seasoning or sauce - we like montreal chicken spice

Steps:

  1. Set the smoker to 375F.
  2. Place the chicken wings on the smoker for 45 minutes.
  3. Check the wings every 10-15 minutes.
  4. Serve with some celery, carrots, and your favorite sauce.
